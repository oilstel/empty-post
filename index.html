Empty Post is a social network for the moment. There is no feed (past or future). Nothing is stored. Post your thoughts and see them disappear into the universe.

Tell me more

We have been busy planning a day no social media called Empty Day on October 12, 2019 and have had a lot of time to think about social media and the energy people put into it. We thought it would be interesting to build a social network that dissipates the energy that is put into it. As you post, nothing is stored, and there's no feed to check. We like to think of it as a social media platform built for the moment!

Some ways of using Empty Post

Try to post something thats been bothering you. Let it fade away. Now try to post something that makes you happy or a self-affirmation. Don't judge what you write. Just notice how you feel after each post.

You could also try to post something nice about yourself every day. Always write in the present tense. Notice how you feel after a week. Do you still need to post here? Do you still need to post on social media?
